Small Multiples Interface

The Small Multiples interface shows several videos in a grid layout. All images or videos can be compared at the same time. An example of this layout can be seen below in Figure 1.

Fig.1. Example of a Small Multiples layout of 9 plant videos.


The system's workspace automatically places videos into a Small Multiples layout. You can play through the videos by pressing SPACEBAR, or by dragging the scrollbar located at the bottom of the page. Watch frame by frame by tapping the LEFT ARROW or the RIGHT ARROW.

You can also zoom in by holding CONTROL and pressing the + key, and zoom out by holding CONTROL and pressing the - key.

If you forget any of the features available, click on the help button located on the left side of the scrollbar.
