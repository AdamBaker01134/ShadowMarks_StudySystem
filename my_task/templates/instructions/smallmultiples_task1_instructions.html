Task 1 - Tallest Plant with Small Multiples


Fig.1. Example of performing small multiples comparison of 6 sunflower plants.

This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 plant growing videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find the plant that grows the tallest.

Play through all the videos and look for the highest point that each plant reaches. Select the plant whose highest point is the tallest.

Reminder: select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.

Click Continue to begin.
