Task 2 - Sea Ice Extent with Shadow Marks


Fig.1. Imaginary dotted red line halfway between the two islands of interest. Find between 1 and 3 videos where the sea ice extends past this imaginary halfway line.

Description: This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 Arctic sea ice videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find between 1 and 3 videos where the ice extends past the imaginary line drawn between the middle of the two islands. This line is visualized in Figure 1.

Steps: First, draw a line halfway between the two islands, like in Figure 1, using the Line tool. If you want to be precise, you can use the Rectangle tool to draw a rectangle around the two islands then use the Line tool to split the rectangle vertically. Then, play though all the videos and look for between 1 and 3 videos where ice (light blue and white) extends past the line. Select all videos where this happens.

Reminder: Select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.

Click Continue to begin.
