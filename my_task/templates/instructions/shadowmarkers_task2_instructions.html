Task 2 - Sea Ice Extent with Shadow Marks


Fig.1. Example of performing a shadow mark comparison the two islands of interest in 1 of 6 sea ice videos.
A rectangle is drawn to encapsulate the two islands, and a line is drawn down its center.

This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 Arctic sea ice videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find between 1 and 3 videos where the ice extends past the vertical halfway line between the two islands contained in the rectangle in Figure 1.

First, make a mark to encapsulate the area of interest (using either the rectangle, line, or freeform tool), like in Figure 1. Then, draw a vertical line down the center of this area, like in Figure 1. Then, play through all the videos and look for between 1 and 3 videos where ice (light blue and white) extends past the middle line. Select all videos where this happens.

Reminder: select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.
