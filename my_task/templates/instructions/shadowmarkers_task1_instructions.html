Task 1 - Tallest Plant with Shadow Marks


Fig.1. Example of performing shadow marker comparison on 6 sunflower plants.

This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 plant videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find the plant that grows the tallest.

Start by playing through each video and guessing which plant reachest the highest point. Make a mark at this point (we recommend a line mark, but you can use any of the mark types available). Then go through each of the other videos and do the following:
  1. Play through the plant video and check if the plant grows higher than your mark.
  2. If it does, remove the last mark and add a new one to the highest point of the new video.
  3. Continue on to the next video.
Once you have gone through all the videos, select the video that the remaining mark belongs to (hovering over the mark will highlight the video it belongs to).

Reminder: select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.

Click Continue to begin.
