Small Multiples Interface

The Small Multiples interface shows several images in a grid layout. All images can be compared at the same time. An example of this layout can be seen below in Figure 1.

Fig.1. Example of a Small Multiples layout of 9 scatterplots.


The system's workspace automatically places images into a Small Multiples layout. You can zoom in by holding CONTROL and pressing the + key, and zoom out by holding CONTROL and pressing the - key.

If you forget any of the features available, click on the help button located in the bottom left corner (as seen in Figure 3).

Fig.3. Help button.


Warning: If you switch tabs, exit fullscreen, or move your cursor out of the webpage for too long, you will redirected to another screen and you will need to restart the task.
