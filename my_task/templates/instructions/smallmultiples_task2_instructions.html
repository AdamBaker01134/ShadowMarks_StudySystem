Task 2 - Sea Ice Extent with Small Multiples


Fig.1. Two islands of interest. Find between 1 and 3 videos where the sea ice extends past the vertical halfway line between the two islands.

This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 Arctic sea ice videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find between 1 and 3 videos where the ice extends past the vertical halfway line between the two islands circled in Figure 1.

Imagine there is a line between the middle of the top island and the bottom island. Play through all the videos and look for between 1 and 3 videos where ice (light blue and white) extends past this line.

Reminder: select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.
