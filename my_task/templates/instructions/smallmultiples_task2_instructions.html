Task 2 - Sea Ice Extent with Small Multiples


Fig.1. Imaginary dotted red line halfway between the two islands of interest. Find between 1 and 3 videos where the sea ice extends past this imaginary halfway line.

Description: This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 Arctic sea ice videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find between 1 and 3 videos where the ice extends past the imaginary line between the two islands. This line is visualized in Figure 1.

Steps: Play through all the videos and look for between 1 and 3 videos where ice (light blue and white) extends past the imaginary line.
