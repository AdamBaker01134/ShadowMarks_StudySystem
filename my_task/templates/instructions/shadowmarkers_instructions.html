TOOLBAR

Toolbars are common in graphical user interfaces. Click on the button corresponding to the tool you require.
Circle
When in circle mode click to being drawing a circle with it's center at the mouse position. Hold the mouse button down and move the mouse to change the circle's diameter. Release the mouse to finish drawing the circle.

Rectangle
When in rectangle mode click to being drawing a rectangle with a corner at the mouse position. Hold the mouse button down and move the mouse to change the rectangles length and width. Release the mouse to finish drawing the rectangle.

Polygon
When in polygon mode click to being drawing a polygon with a vertex at the mouse position. Click where you want to add a vertex, and click on the initial vertex to complete the polygon.

Move
When in move mode, hover over a shape and click to "grab" it. Hold the mouse button down and move the mouse to change the shape's position. Release the mouse to finish moving the shape.

Resize
When in scale mode, hover over a shape and click to scale it. Hold the mouse button down and move the mouse to change the shape's size. Release the mouse to finish scaling the shape.

Rotate
When in rotate mode, hover over a shape and click to rotate it. Hold the mouse button down and move the mouse to change the shape's rotation. Release the mouse to finish rotating the shape.

Handle
When in handle grabbing mode click and hold on the handle you want to grab. Drag the mouse to move the handle and release the mouse when you are satisfied

Colour
This will popup a colourwheel on the top left of the screen. Click on your desired colour to select a colour and click on shapes to paint them the selected colour.

Send to top
When in send to top mode, clicking on a shape will bring it to the top.

Send to bottom
When in send to bottom mode, clicking on a shape will send it to the bottom.

Erase
When in erase mode, clicking on a shape will erase it.

Copy
When in copy mode, clicking on a shape will create a copy of it.
