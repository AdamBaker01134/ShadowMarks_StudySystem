Task 1 - Tallest Plant with Overlays


Fig.1. Example of performing overlay comparison of 6 sunflower plants.

This task has 2 comparison trials. For each trial, 6 plant growing videos will load in for you to view and compare. Your task is to find the plant that grows the tallest.

Start by adding the first video to the overlay (click on it). Then go through each of the other videos and do the following:
  1. Add the video to the overlay (eye will open with 2 as its number).
  2. Play through the overlaid plant videos to see which grows the tallest.
  3. Remove the shorter plant from the overlay by clicking on it on the left (eye will close).
  4. Continue on to the next video.
Once you have gone through all the videos, select the video that is still in the overlay (only video with its eye open).

Reminder: select a video by holding CONTROL and clicking on it.

Click Continue to begin.
